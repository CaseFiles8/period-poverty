Period Poverty



Besides the gender pay gap, women face a health disparity in which the simple existence in a female body requires sacrifices that male counterparts can completely bypass. Without even mentioning the consequences of period cramps, period products are both inconvenient to access and pose an extra cost that women need to account for in each month's budget. Once period cramps are brought into the picture, women face having to use sick hours to recuperate and risk looking "unreliable" at work.
